Step 1: The "MoMo-Linked" Monetization Model

For bloggers in 2026, the most resilient income stream is Recurring SaaS Affiliation. Unlike one-time bounties, these programs pay you every month the user stays subscribed.[3]

  • Jasper AI: 30% recurring (first 12 months).[4]
  • Surfer SEO: 25% lifetime recurring commissions.[5]
  • Digital Product Overlays: Sell PDF guides or prompt libraries using Gumroad or Lemon Squeezy overlays to bypass GitHub's commercial hosting limits.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much do beginner bloggers earn in 2026?

While most earn $0-$50 in the first 6 months (the Google Sandbox phase), sites focused on high-intent AI niches often hit $300-$500/month within a year through affiliate growth.[6]

Can I monetize a.github.io subdomain?

Yes. GitHub permits ads (AdSense) and affiliate links as long as the site remains a legitimate project and not a primary e-commerce store.
