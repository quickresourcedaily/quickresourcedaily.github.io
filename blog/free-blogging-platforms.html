How to Create a Website for Free: The 10 Best Platforms Ranked

Updated March 23, 2026 • 15 Min Read

In 2026, you no longer need money to build a professional website. What matters now is choosing the right platform based on your goal — traffic, control, or monetization.

⚡ Quick Summary:
  • Control: GitHub Pages
  • Traffic: Medium
  • Money: Blogger

Choose Your Goal First

Before building, define your goal. Google ranks based on trust and structure, not just content.

#1

GitHub Pages

The best option for full control and long-term SEO growth.

✔ Free forever
✔ Full control
✔ Fast loading
✖ Technical setup
✖ No CMS by default
💡 Strategy:

Use GitHub Pages as your main site and scale into authority SEO over time.

#2

Medium

A built-in audience platform that can drive instant traffic.

✔ Instant reach
✔ High authority
✔ Easy writing
✖ No control
✖ Limited monetization
💡 Strategy:

Use Medium for traffic, then link back to your main site.

#3

Blogger

Best for beginners who want to monetize quickly.

✔ AdSense friendly
✔ Easy setup
✔ Google-backed
✖ Outdated UI
✖ Limited flexibility
💡 Strategy:

Focus on publishing consistent SEO articles to grow traffic fast.

SEO Structure That Works

  • Simple Navigation: Keep everything within 1 click
  • Mobile First: Most traffic is mobile
  • Authority Pages: Add About + Contact

My Pro Tip

Build your main site on GitHub Pages and use Medium to drive traffic. This builds backlinks and authority faster.
